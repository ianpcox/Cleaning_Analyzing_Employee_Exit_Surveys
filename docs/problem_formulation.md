# Problem Formulation – Cleaning_Analyzing_Employee_Exit_Surveys

## Research / business question

Do short-tenure vs. long-tenure employees differ in stated reasons for leaving, and what segments show elevated exit risk or dissatisfaction?

## Primary success metric and threshold

- **Proportions by segment** (tenure, age, etc.); odds ratios or simple logistic regression for dissatisfaction.
- **Baseline:** Overall exit reasons distribution.

## Stakeholders and decisions

- **HR:** Decisions about retention and intervention. **Product vision:** Exit analytics for HR (anonymized dashboards, optional retention alerts).
